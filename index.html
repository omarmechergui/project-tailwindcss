The Beginning After the End:

The adaptation will be directed by Keitaro Motonaga (Digimon Adventure tri., DATE A LIVE) at the animation studio Studio A-CAT has confirmed its acquisition to stream the series in 2025!

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is a dark fantasy manga series that has captivated audiences worldwide. Set in a world where humanity lives within fortified cities to protect themselves from gigantic humanoid creatures known as Titans, the story follows a group of young soldiers who vow to exterminate the Titans and reclaim their freedom. The series delves into complex themes of war, loss, and the nature of humanity, while delivering stunning visuals and intense action sequences. With its intricate plot, unforgettable characters, and thought-provoking narrative, Attack on Titan has become a cultural phenomenon.
