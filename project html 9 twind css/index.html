about

HoYoverse is a global video game and entertainment company headquartered in Shanghai, China. It was founded in 2012 as miHoYo and rebranded to HoYoverse in 2021. The company is known for developing and publishing popular games such as Genshin Impact, Honkai Impact 3rd, Honkai: Star Rail, Tears of Themis, and Zenless Zone Zero. HoYoverse is committed to providing immersive virtual world experiences to players around the world and fostering a strong sense of community among its fans.
